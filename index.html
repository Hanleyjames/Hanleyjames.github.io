A little about James

Hello there, in short I am a Software Engineer located north of Seattle. Originally I studied and passed an IT program in Highschool in Florida. There I was trained in COMPTIA A+ certification. For my senior year project, I created maps for the old Half-Life 2 Source Engine. I took a break from the tech field and persued some other interest of mine. In college, I persued a PoliSci and Philosophy degree. I studied at the University of Central Florida. After a long discussion with a few professors and teachers (who told me that I would do best if I got two PHD's), I decided to go back to working in the tech field.

This leads me to where I am today! After studying and working with Ruby on Rails and doing Lab Tech work at Microsoft, I decided to take it seriousl. I worked with Epicodus, and started an internship with DTOOR, where I helped with Front End development and deployment. After that I worked as a part time T.A. for the University of Washington with Trillogy. There I assisted with their MERN stack bootcamp program. Afterwords I worked with PTG and helped with interal and external projects. This gave me a lot of experience in working with multiple clients and their unique demands. Each problem comes with a different stack and solution. Each a wonderful chance for me to learn. During my time as a Software Engineer I have worked with start ups, large enterprises and currently at a co-op part time. The wonderful experience of working at a co-op is the horizontal team organization and communication. The benefits of these experiences, is that I have learned how to deal with many different teams of all sizes.
